About us

History

Nyakiambi girls secondary school started in 1968 as a mixed secondary school. It became a girls only school in 1988, it was two streamed until 2002 when the third stream was added. The forth stream was introduced in 2009. In 2018, the fifth stream was added. It is an Extra-County School with over 800 students.

Geographically

The physical location is in Mirangine Sub-County, Nyandarua County
